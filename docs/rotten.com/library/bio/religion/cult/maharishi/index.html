Maharishi Mahesh Yogi

Big believer in vedic flying, which is a total fraud. Briefly a mentor to The Beatles and to a lesser extent the Rolling Stones. Wants to change the world by having governments pay him to practice large scale transcendental meditation (TM), to the point of taking out full page advertisements in news magazines during the 1980's.
