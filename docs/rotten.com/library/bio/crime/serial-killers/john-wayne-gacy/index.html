John Wayne Gacy

John Wayne Gacy was a pillar of his community. He would dress up as Pogo the Clown to entertain children and owned his own local business. He also liked to strangle young men and store bodies under his house. Recently, he's gotten a second career as a artist, making paintings in his jail cell. Adapt or die!

When police officers asked Gacy about a two-by-four affixed with handcuffs they found in his home, he replied that he got the idea from Elmer Wayne Henley.
