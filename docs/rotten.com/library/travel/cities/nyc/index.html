New York City

  • World Trade Center, largest terrorist attack in history.
  • Mafia: Carlo Gambino, Paul Castellano, John Gotti, et al.
  • Son of Sam killer David Berkowitz.
  • Home of fake news sources Saturday Night Live and The Daily Show.
  • John Lennon assassinated in New York City by Mark David Chapman.
  • One of three cities in Las Vegas to have casinos representing it.
  • Three major blackouts.
